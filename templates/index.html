Research Focus

Planetary Exploration

Remote sensing applications in planetary exploration, focusing on spectroscopic analysis of terrestrial and extraterrestrial geological formations.

Petrophysical Modeling

Advanced rock physics modeling for petroleum exploration, integrating laboratory measurements with field-scale applications.

Thermal Spectroscopy

Thermal infrared emission and reflectance spectroscopy for geological characterization and mineral identification.

Hyperspectral Analysis

Hyperspectral remote sensing techniques for mineral exploration and geological mapping applications.

Hydrogeological Systems

Remote sensing and GIS applications in hydrogeological studies, groundwater resource assessment, and sustainable water management.
